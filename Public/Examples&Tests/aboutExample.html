We believe that relationships are the starting point for peace.

SE7EN FAST is that starting point.

We created SE7EN FAST in order to make space for non-Muslims and Muslims to begin ongoing life changing and life giving relationships across lines of religious and cultural difference. Now more than ever, we need to embrace our similarities, wrestle with our differences, and create a story in which we discover the pathway of peace together.
